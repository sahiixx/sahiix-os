## HANDLING OBJECTIONS

#### "I've been busy"

**[AGENT]**

```
Same here — Dubai keeps you moving. That's why I'm not asking for a meeting. Just a voice note. You listen on your own time. If something clicks, we talk. If not, you're not committed to anything.
```

#### "The market is uncertain right now"

**[AGENT]**

```
That's actually why I'm calling. Uncertainty creates opportunities for people who have conviction. I'm not here to convince you — I just want to make sure that if something genuinely good comes across my desk, you're aware of it. You decide what to do with it.
```

#### "I have an agent already"

**[AGENT]**

```
Good — you should. I'm not trying to replace anyone. But here's the thing — I work with a specific segment, and I come across opportunities that most agents don't have access to. 

Keep me as a backup. When your current agent runs dry, give me a call. The worst case is you ignore me. The best case is you find something great.
```

#### "I'm not interested"

**[AGENT]**

```
Noted — I won't keep pushing. Just one ask: if your situation changes or you hear about something worth acting on, reach out. I can help with things most agents can't — mortgage structuring, off-market deals, Golden Visa documentation. 

My number is +971585476077. Take care.
```
